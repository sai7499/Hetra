ANNEXURE 1 - FAQ’s

1. What happens if my cheque does not get cleared?

The following charges will have to be borne by you:

  • Cheque dishonour charges of Rs.500 for every instance of dishonour.
  • Additional finance charges @ 3% per month calculated up to the date of repayment.
  • If you visit any nearest Equitas Branch for making the payment, you will not be charged with Field Visit Charges.
  • If our executive visits you for collecting over dues, you will be charged a sum of Rs.250 as Field Visit Charges.

2. Can I exchange or swap my cheques?

Yes, you can exchange or swap cheques if you have changed your bank or you want your loan to be paid out from a different account you hold. In such cases you will have to send us the following, 15 days before the next cheque is due for clearing:

  • A request with the reason for exchange of cheques.
  • Fresh cheques filled and signed by you for the remaining tenure of the loan.

Please note that this service attracts fee as per rules of the company, in force from time to time. This fee may be paid by way of demand draft / cash in favour of Equitas Finance Private Limited to the nearest branch office.

3. Can I Pre-Pay or foreclose my loan?

Yes, you can. The outstanding loan amount, with pre-payment charges on the principal outstanding and overdue if any, can be settled at least 15 days before your next cheque is due for clearing. The termination papers and unused cheques will be sent to you after the closure of the loan.

4. What do I do if there is a change in address or I am transferred?

  • If the address change / transfer is within the State you are residing in, please send a written request with proof such as: Ration card, Voter ID, Telephone Bill, Electricity Bill etc., to our Head office at Chennai or our branch closest to you, to update our records. In case of change in your bank, please follow instructions as per item no.2.
  • If the address change / transfer is outside the state you are currently residing in, then you will have to contact our branch nearest to you for completing the formalities related to your vehicle registration etc. In case of a change in your bank, please follow instructions as per item no.2.

5. Do I get receipts for all the payments made?

Since the cheques are cleared through the normal banking system no receipts are issued. However, if cash payments are made by you due to certain unavoidable circumstances, please insist and ensure that you collect a receipt for such payments made to the company.

6. I am an NPDC customer. Do I get a receipt for all the EMI’s paid?

  • Yes. You will get receipts for all the EMI’s paid by you.
  • If paid on due date at the branch as agreed, you need not pay any field visit charges. However, if you have opted for our Relationship Officer / Field Staff visiting you, Rs.150/- will be charged towards field visit charges. Further, for every visit beyond the due date Rs.250/- will be charged towards Field Visit Charges.

7. What do I do if I have a problem in repaying the EMI?

Be in touch with the branch and discuss about your problem with the Branch Manager who will help you in giving a mutually agreed and fair solution.

8. How do I get a statement of account?

Company will send you a Statement of Account every year, which is free of cost. If you need a statement in the interim period please contact your service branch with a written request; you will be required to pay Rs.500/- for every such request.
